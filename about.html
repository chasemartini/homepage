About

Currently, I am a Multimedia Artist, specializing in Audiovisual Production. Other than working on a TV series, several TV commercials and promotional videos, plus one viral advertizement, I have worked in many other fields, ranging from my current day job in logistics, to the hospitality industry.

However, my most relevant work to coding has been my roles in the B2B and B2C sales world, working for technology-based companies. During my time living in London, I worked at CCA International selling broadband, TV, and telephone products and packages on behalf of British Telecom. Then I got a job at the now non-existent KGB Deals website, which was part of the online "daily deals" market, similar to the popular website run by Groupon.

Now that I have discovered Coding and Web Development, these collective experiences have provided me with a platform to really expand on, because I can incorporate all of their aspects into practical ideas in a digital format to create web content and provide a unique and interactive user experience!

  • Reading books, magazines, and comics
