Goal

With this website, I plan to not only document the progress of my web development skills, but will also try to answer the question of whether Malcolm Gladwell's 10,000 hours theory is true or not in the context of coding. In his book, "Outliers: The Story of Success," Gladwell states that it takes 10,000 hours to master, or acheive world-class expertise in any skill.

I understand that this type of challenge is completely relative to what you are learning, both in it's complexity, and also the scope of the subject that is being studied. However, I believe that although one could put an endless amount of hours into any craft, to become a "master" of your field, it is very much achievable within the 10,000 hour estimate... and I think that you can actually reach your learning target much faster than what you might imagine.

So please follow my progress and check back on this site regularly to track my learning and improvements.

Thanks for visiting and please check out the other sections :-)
